Got Questions?

FAQ

Is there a registration fee?+
Yes. The registration fee is ₹99 for Early Bird and ₹149 for Regular Entry. Early registration is recommended as slots are limited.
Do I need to be a professional musician to attend?+
Not at all. Gaana Bajaana is open to everyone — from beginners to experienced performers. If you love music, you belong here.
Can I bring my own instrument?+
Yes, you are welcome to bring instruments such as guitar, keyboard, tabla, cajon, etc. However, it is not mandatory. Bring them only if you already have them.
How will I receive my entry pass after registration?+
Once you complete the registration, you will receive a confirmation email with your entry pass. If you do not receive it within 1 hour, please check your spam/promotions folder or contact us via WhatsApp.
Can I attend with a group of friends?+
Yes, groups are welcome. However, each attendee must register individually and carry their own entry pass.
Will food be provided during the event?+
Food is not included in the registration. Drinking water will be available at the venue. You can order food at the venue at your own expense.
How can I stay updated about event details and future editions?+
Join our WhatsApp community for real-time updates, announcements, and collaborations. You can also follow us on Instagram for event highlights and future event information.
