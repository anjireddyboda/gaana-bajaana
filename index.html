Got Questions?

FAQ

Is registration free?+
Yes! Gaana Bajaana is completely free to attend. Just fill the registration form, receive your QR pass, and show it at entry.
Do I need to be a professional musician?+
Absolutely not! Gaana Bajaana welcomes everyone — from total beginners to seasoned performers. The only requirement is a love for music.
Can I bring my instrument?+
Yes, please do! Guitars, keyboards, tabla, cajon — all welcome. Basic equipment like mics and amps will be provided at the venue.
How do I get my QR pass after registering?+
After submitting the form, you'll receive a confirmation with your QR pass. If you don't see it in 24 hours, check spam or reach out on WhatsApp.
Can I come with a group of friends?+
Of course! Groups are welcome. Each person needs their own QR pass, so make sure everyone registers individually.
Will there be food and beverages?+
Light refreshments will be available. Stay tuned to the WhatsApp community group for specific details closer to the event.
How can I stay updated about future events?+
Join our WhatsApp community group for real-time updates and to find jam partners. Also follow us on Instagram!
